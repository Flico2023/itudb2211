Welcome to PLStats

PLStads is a web application for performing CRUD (Create-Read-Update-Delete) operations with the data of the English football league, the premier league.

Using PLStads, you can process the attack, defense and goal data of the teams, in addition, you can add or delete teams in the league.

PLStats was developed by Mehmet Emre Topdal, Barış Kerem Hüseyinoğlu and Bilge Kır for ITU BLG317 course

in the development of the project, Python/Flask, WTForms SQL and Bootstrap are used
